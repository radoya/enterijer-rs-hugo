## Nenad Radoja — editor

Nenad Radoja is the founder and editor of Enterijer.rs. Through his own renovation projects and work with contractors in Serbia, he learned what actually works in practice — and shares those lessons in practical articles on this site.

## Editorial policy

- **Human editing** — every article is reviewed and edited by a human before publishing.
- **Original photography** — we use photos from real projects whenever possible.
- **First-hand experience** — advice is based on practical experience, not rewritten content.
- **Corrections** — spotted an error? Let us know via the [contact form](/kontakt/) and we will fix it right away.
